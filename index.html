BombSquda: Mell's Modpack

BombSquda is a Modpack for BombSquad that introduces a bunch of gameplay changes that will either make you scream of rage or joy.

It introduces 2 new maps, a whole new cast of characters, a new gamemode (and 2 slightly altered ones), various mechanics, 3 new powerups, a whole alteration to the vanilla assets, and more! So, what are you waiting for? Go play it!!!!!! NOW!!!!!!!!!!
