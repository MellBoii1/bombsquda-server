---------------- SpazBot ----------------

Ever wanted to check stuff related to BombSquda,

or just want a bot to fill in your server that's pretty empty?

SpazBot can help with that!

------------------------------------------

SpazBot is a bot for discord that allows you to track and view the leaderboard where players race for getting the best times on co-op levels, do a few fun commands, and more up-coming stuff!

It is still in active development, which means more commands, features, and other things might roll-out anytime!

If you wish to add the bot to your server, click the button below!
